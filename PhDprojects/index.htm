PhD-project supervision

Please contact me at if you are interested in starting a possible project under my supervision. I welcome applications from potential PhD candidates in the following broad areas:
- human-computer interaction, including information security, information privacy, user-experience, personal information management, virtual reality and technology acceptance;
- judgement and decision-making;
- modelling in applied and basic psychology research;
- measurement in applied and basic psychology;
- applications of psychology in other domains.
